About Us

Cookie jar is a media tech startup that focuses on creating great content online and offline. Content, being the ultimate goal, the startup is trying to redefine how it is created and how it is put out there for the people to consume. From summits to interviews, and exhibitions to articles or images online, we focus on tell it a great story in the field of entrepreneurship, technology and art so that people can be truly educated and can make a difference.
